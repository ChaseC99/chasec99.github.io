About

Hello there - welcome to my website!

My name is Chase Carnaroli and I am an aspiring Software Engineer currently studying Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine.

Back in middle school, after seeing a "Learn to Code" campaign, I was inspired to start programming. Through Khan Academy's computer programming course, I learned the basics of computer science. From there, I started teaching myself programming on and off until my senior year of high school. It was then that AP Computer Science was offered for the first time at my school. I had a fantastic teacher that year who not only gave me a great foundation for programming, but also helped me fall in love with the field of computer science. After taking that class, I knew that I wanted to major in Computer Science and become a software engineer.

Since then, I have made several Java applications, a handful of websites, an iOS app, and more - all of which can be seen on my "Projects" page! I am now profient in Python, C++, Swift, Java, SQL, and JavaScript; although I am constantly trying to learn new languages and build more cool stuff.

When I’m not programming, I’m most likely spending time out in nature either hiking, hammocking, playing beach volleyball, or watching the sunset.

I have built this website from scratch to show off of my work and to hopefully connect with some cool people. Feel free to reach out and say hello!
